Aggelos Biboudis

Postdoctoral Researcher

My name is Aggelos Biboudis and I’m a postdoctoral researcher at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne. I am a member of LAMP led by Martin Odersky.

I have a PhD from the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications at the University of Athens, under the supervision of Yannis Smaragdakis in programming languages. More specifically my thesis proposes mechanisms to make streams extensible and performant, while maintaining their high level structure.

You can find bits of my work under my github page. I tweet about PL, computer science and geek stuff. Ping me at aggelos.biboudis att epfl.ch or LinkedIn.
