Aggelos Biboudis

PhD candidate

My name is Aggelos Biboudis and I’m a PhD student at the Dept. of Informatics and Telecommunications, University of Athens, under the supervision of Yannis Smaragdakis since July 2012. I am interested mostly in: modular extensibility, EDSLs, metaprogramming (staging) and stream oriented programming.

You can find bits of my work under my Github page. I tweet about PL, computer science and geek stuff @biboudis. Ping me at biboudis att di.uoa.gr or LinkedIn.
